Description

Abstract / Description:
Dataset WM1796 is the Department of Natural Resources, Mines and Energy (DNRM) Surface Water Quantity Monitoring Network 2015-16. For more information see 'Water monitoring and data' (http://www.qld.gov.au/environment/water/quality/monitoring ) and the Water Monitoring Information Portal (https://water-monitoring.information.qld.gov.au ).
Purpose:
Legislation: 2000 Act Number 34, "Water Act 2000", section 35 "Information recording and planning", (b) "... must provide information for planning purposes by - " (i) "regularly measuring and keeping publicly available records of the volume and quality of water in Queensland ..." (Internet http://www.legislation.qld.gov.au/OQPChome.htm http://www.legislation.qld.gov.au/LEGISLTN/CURRENT/W/WaterA00.pdf ).
Additional Information:
Metadata format: International Standards Organisation (ISO) 19139 Geographic Information - Metadata - XML schema implementation of ISO 19115 Geographic Information - Metadata. (Queensland Spatial Catalogue (QSpatial) metadata is supplied as .htm and .xml files.) Information asset theme: Water Management, sub-theme: monitor, surface water, quantity. Attributes: IDENTIFIER Gauging station unique identifier. TITLE Gauging station name. LATITUDE LONGITUDE Location of the station instrumentation hut (decimal degrees, Geocentric Datum of Australia 1994, GDA94). BASIN Drainage basin. URLSTATION, URLBASIN Uniform Resource Locator of the station's and the station's basin's webpages in the Water Monitoring Information Portal. More information is available on the Portal including live data, details of records available, time periods of measurements and water quality parameters that are available. (Reference: CAS1922.1)
